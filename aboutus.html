Nnamie Oil and Gas Nigeria Limited

NNAMIE OIL & GAS NIGERIA LIMITED is a dynamic and forward-thinking company committed to delivering top-tier solutions across the Power and Energy sector, Civil Construction, Pipeline Surveillance and Security, as well as procurement services.
Founded on principles of integrity, efficiency, and innovation, we pride ourselves on executing projects with a strong focus on safety, quality, and client satisfaction.
With a team of experienced professionals and a deep understanding of Nigeria's industrial landscape, we provide reliable services that support sustainable infrastructure development and energy delivery.
At NNAMIE, we are driven by a vision to be a trusted partner in powering growth and protecting assets, while meeting the evolving needs of both public and private sector clients.

Our Vision

To be a leading indigenous service provider in Power, Energy, Civil Construction, and Security infrastructure—recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable development across Nigeria and beyond.


Our Mission


To deliver safe, efficient, and high-quality services in the areas of power and energy, civil construction, pipeline security, and procurement—leveraging our expertise, technology, and people to exceed client expectations and contribute meaningfully to national growth
